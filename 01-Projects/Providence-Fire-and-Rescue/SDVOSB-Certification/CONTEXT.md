# SDVOSB Certification

Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business certification application for Providence Fire & Rescue through the SBA. Required for government contracting eligibility.

## Key Details
- Certifying body: SBA (Small Business Administration)
- Designation: SDVOSB — Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business
- Purpose: unlock federal government contracting eligibility
- Status: application in progress
